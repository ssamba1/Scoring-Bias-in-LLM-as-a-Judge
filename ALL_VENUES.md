## Tier 1: Preprint Servers (Guaranteed, Immediate)

| Venue | Time | Cost | What You Get | Deadline |
|-------|------|------|-------------|----------|
| **arXiv** (cs.CL) | 30 min | $0 | DOI, citable, timestamped priority | Open |
| **Zenodo** (via GitHub) | 5 min | $0 | DOI, indexed, connects to repo | Open |
| **Preprints.org** | 10 min | $0 | DOI, indexed | Open |
| **OSF Preprints** | 10 min | $0 | DOI, preregistration if desired | Open |
| **ResearchGate** | 5 min | $0 | PDF upload, indexed | Open |

**Strategy:** arXiv first. If endorsement is an issue, Zenodo in 5 min.

## The Optimal Strategy

```
TODAY:
  1. arXiv preprint (30 min, guaranteed)
  2. Create ISEF account, note deadlines

THIS MONTH:
  3. Apply to regional ISEF fair
  4. Write Medium blog post explaining the finding
  5. Prepare NeurIPS HS Track submission (deadline June 2027)

THIS YEAR:
  6. Submit to NeurIPS HS Track
  7. If rejected → submit to ACL Findings
  8. If accepted → camera-ready + press
```

**Total venues you can apply to simultaneously:**
arXiv + ISEF + NeurIPS HS Track + blog post = **4 concurrent submissions, zero conflicts.**
