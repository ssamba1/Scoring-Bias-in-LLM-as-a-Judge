How We Fill Their Gap

Li et al. wrote: "The underlying causes of these scoring biases remain to be validated."

We answered this. By testing 44 model families with both base and instruct variants, we showed that scoring bias is modulated by instruction tuning and the effect is differential (format biases improve, content biases worsen). This is the first such demonstration in the literature.
