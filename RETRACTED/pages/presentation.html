Interpretation

When position bias AND verbosity bias co-occur:

Llama 3: 2.10× worse than expected

Individual biases predict 0.42 drop. Actual: 0.71 drop.

What this means

  • Single-bias tests underestimate real-world bias
  • Worst-case evaluation items are 2× more degraded
  • Model selection affects interaction profiles

What to do about it

  • Test bias combinations
  • Apply worst-case analysis
  • Fix instruction tuning, not pre-training
Slide 8 of 12

Why This Matters

🔬 For AI Safety

Biased judges approving unsafe outputs short + negative responses (common for harmful content) get double-penalized.

📊 For Benchmarks

Every published benchmark using AI judges may have systematic biases. Some models may appear worse than they are.

🎯 For Practitioners

Include base model controls. Target mitigation at instruction tuning. Test bias combinations.

📖 For Researchers

Bias interactions are the most impactful open problem. Also: cross-cultural bias, temporal stability, judge fatigue.
