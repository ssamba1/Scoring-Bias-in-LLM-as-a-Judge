## How to Frame the AI Usage

STS allows AI in research with disclosure. Your application should say:

> *"AI tools (large language models) were used to assist in code generation for data analysis and initial drafting of certain sections of the research report. All experimental design, data collection, analysis, interpretation, and conclusions were performed independently by the author. AI use is detailed in the acknowledgments."*

This is honest and acceptable. STS wants to know YOU did the thinking — not that you hand-wrote every line of code.
